Who is this for?

Below are some common problems:

Do you want to legally perform work over $500?

Are you looking to legally perform construction work that exceeds $500, including both labor and materials? Obtaining your California contractor’s license is the best investment for securing your future in the construction industry.

Are you an experienced journeyman?

Do you have 4 years of journeyman level experience in the trade and aspire to start a solo career? We can help you obtain your contractor’s license in as little as two months, empowering you to work independently.

Are you skilled in your trade but dislike dealing with the paperwork?

Our program guides you through the application process, letting you focus on your craft while we handle the bureaucratic details.

Dreaming of starting your own construction company?

A contractor’s license is essential and in most cases required. We offer the resources and guidance to help you launch your business successfully. Make it official and enhance your credibility by obtaining your license through our proven preparation courses.

Have you been doing construction work for a while without a license?

The CSLB accepts self-employment experience, provided you have the paper
trail to prove at least 4  years. 

Do you want to add a classification to your existing contractors license?

Our program will guide you through adding a classification to your current contractor's license, helping you expand your scope of work.
